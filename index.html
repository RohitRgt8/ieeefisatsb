About IEEE FISAT SB.

The Student Branch Chapter of FISAT was contoured in 2003 with the motto, "Integrating Innovations", aimed at providing technical skill sets to the scholars and enhancing the standards of the activities in the society we serve. Shri S Gopakumar, the Student's Activities Chairman and IEEE GOLD MEMBER Shri Vargheese Cherian took the initial steps to plant the roots of the World's largest technical and professional organization at FISAT. Numerous events and programs are hosted by IEEE FISAT SBC within the campus, including Tech talks, Management Sessions, Workshops, Seminars and so on. Our chapter has been rated among the top 5 active student branches in the Kerala section. Currently, the chapter has a membership count of 460. Furthermore, our SB registers more than 100 new IEEE student members each year.

  • Outstanding Student Branch IEEE India Council 2022.
  • IEEE Region 10 Exemplerary Student Branch Award 2022.
  • Darrel Chong Bronze Medal for the activity Student Development Program (SDP).
  • 2nd Largest in IEEE Kerala Section.
  • 2nd Largest Computer Society and Power and Energy Society Chapters Globally Membership-wise.
